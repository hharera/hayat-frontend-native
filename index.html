Our Services

We are provide various services to individuals or communities in need. These services include donation and needs , such as food donation , medicine , housing, clothes and books . or Blood Need.

Donations

Our Donations can take many forms, including medicines donations, Food donations of goods or services, clothes Donation and more . Our charitable organizations also offer online donation platform that allow donors to contribute easily and securely .

Needs

Our Needs service in typically involves a comprehensive assessment process to determine the specific needs of each individual or family. Once the needs have been identified, We provide direct assistance such as food banks, property , medicine, books and clothes .

Donations

Donations in
HAYAT-EG

Medicin Donation

We try to improve access to vital medicines for those in need. As a member of our team, you will be making a direct and meaningful impact

Food Donation

Donating food is an easy and impactful way to help those in need. With your help, we can provide nutritious meals to individuals and families who may be struggling to make ends meet.

Clothes Donation

Donating clothes is an excellent way to give back to your community and help those who may not have the means to purchase new clothing. With your help, we can provide warmth, comfort, and dignity to individuals and families in need.

Books Donation

Book Donation is a great way to promote literacy and education. It helps to provide access to books for those who may not have the means to purchase them. Donated books can also help to build community and foster a love of reading.

Properties Donation

Donating property is a generous act that can benefit both the donor and the recipient.
